Welcome to Ese Snap!

Instructions

  • The Pack : The standard 52-card pack is used.
  • 1 player: Player and CPU will have 26 cards each.
  • Objective: Win all the cards.
  • How to play:
  1. Press the card in the centre to play your hand
  2. The computer will then play their hand.
  3. Once two cards of the same number(rank)

    are played click the snap button below.
  4. An alert on the top right will indicate who was the fastest.
  5. The game will continue until a deck has been emptied.
  6. The outcome will lead you to either

    the winners or losers page.
  7. Good luck!
